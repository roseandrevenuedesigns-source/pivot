Next-Quarter Containment Direction

Choose the next-quarter direction based on what created the most pressure this quarter. If core bills stayed covered but flexibility stayed tight, focus on strengthening buffers. If accounts slipped or remained behind, focus on bringing accounts current. If income covered the month but not consistently beyond it, focus on restoring sufficiency. If the system held but still felt fragile, focus on maintaining containment or reducing sequencing pressure. You do not need to fix everything at once. Containment goals can be sequenced over time.

Quarterly changes often appear in structure before they appear in scores. Containment progress is still progress. Stability is directional, not instant.
